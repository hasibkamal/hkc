PRIMARY SCHOOL LIFE

Primary School Life

My primary school's name is Experimental School Attached to P.T.I. Brahmanbaria. My school was not so far from my home. My primary school life was the best part of my life. It is the opening page of my educational life with lot of golden moments.


I can easily remember all stories of my primary school life. When I think about my primary school life then I easily get a fresh imagination of all past memories. In our school we had a big play ground. We had played various kinds of game in our playground.

HIGH SCHOOL LIFE

High School Life

I was In Annada Govt High School from class 6 to 8. And after that I admitted in Brahmanbaria Technical School and Collage. I have got a lot of good friends in Technical School. It was a new experience of my life. I had to adopted myself with a new environment which gave me a lot of experience .


It was a Technical School so there was no science, arts or business studies as like as general education. There are total four trade in this school and my trade was Computer Trade. I had seen Windows-97, Windows-98 operating system in Computers. It was the beginning of my interest on Computer.

POLYTECHNIC LIFE

Polytechnic Life

I've Completed Diploma in Engineering on Computer Technology from Brahmanbaria Polytechnic Institute. Polytechnic life was the most learning step of my life. It was the beginning of my freedom life.


We were five friends at the beginning of a different life when we were far from our family. At the beginning we were sufferer with lot problems. But these problems gave us valuable lessons for life. I miss my Polytechnic life and my friends so much.

UNIVERSITY LIFE

University Life

I've admited at Daffodil International University after completing Diploma graduation. I have got a lot of good friends in my varsity. Boyes are really very good and cooprative.


I am spending very good times with my friends. I am very lucky to have them.
